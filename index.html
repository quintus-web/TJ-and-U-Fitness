Our Story

WHERE GOALS
BECOME REALITY

TJ & U Fitness Club is Buru Buru's newest and most exciting fitness destination — a space engineered for people who take their health seriously. From the moment you walk in, you'll feel the difference.

Whether you're building muscle, shedding weight, improving endurance, or just starting out — our certified coaches guide every step. Your body type, your diet, your discipline, your goals — all matter here.

"As you train towards a specific fitness goal, your diet contributes a whole lot. Understand your body type and with discipline, develop a proper eating habit that goes hand in hand with the target goal you're aiming at."
